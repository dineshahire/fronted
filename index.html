What is anime? If you aren’t already a fan, you may be asking this question. To start, anime is short for animation and is an art form that originated in Japan. The word literally means “animated cartoon” in Japanese, and while everything in Japan is considered anime, outside the country the genre is very specific. The descriptor essentially refers to all cartoons originating in Japan or taking on signature aspects and style of Japanese animation.
